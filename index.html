What is this?

A personal lab where I put my data science and AI engineering work into practice. The centerpiece is the AI Trading Arena — a virtual trading competition where $10,000 starting capital and $500 weekly deposits are managed by four independent strategies: an LLM-based AI Analyst, a four-vote composite quant system (MA/RSI/Bollinger/Random Forest), and a DCA baseline. All signals are generated, executed, and tracked automatically via a daily data pipeline powered by the OpenClaw agent platform. This is not financial advice — it is a sandbox for testing statistical thinking in live markets.
